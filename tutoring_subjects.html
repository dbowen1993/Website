Tutoring Subjects

Mathematics is a subject that we cover throughout the grade levels and into the college and university levels. In terms of basic math, we can support you or your child from kindergarten through grade 12. We can assist you in algebra, whether it be introductory algebra or pre-calculus. If you have concerns about calculus, we cover introductory calculus at the high school level as well as calculus I and calculus II. For statistics, we can support you in the statistics courses that are offered in a wide variety of programs at colleges and universities.

Economics is generally taught at higher levels of education, and it is broadly divided between macroeconomics and microeconomics. Between the two, microeconomics is considered to be more mathematically intensive and concrete, while macroeconomics focuses more heavily on theoretical knowledge and comprehension. Whether you struggle with one or the other, or both, we can help to ensure your success by identifying your strengths and bolstering your weaknesses in the aforementioned subcategories.

English Language Arts, like any language, can be difficult for somebody learning it as a second language. We have recognized the increasing demand for second language education in English. We have a tutor with pedagogical training in teaching phonetics and the syntax of the English language. When it comes to English as a first language, assistance is readily available for you or your child at any grade level and into the college and university levels. Advanced essay writing in particular is a vital skill for all students in higher education, regardless of their chosen discipline.

Test Preparation is a need that we at Triumvirate Tutors understand all too well. Sometimes, you just urgently require help in passing or aceing that exam. We will do our best to help you excel, and will tailor our tutoring for teaching in accordance to the material of that test or examination. Prepare to pass with flying colours!
